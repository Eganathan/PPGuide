# Expenses Action

Track all costs and expenditures related to your farming activities.

## Purpose
Monitor spending on inputs, labor, equipment, maintenance, and other operational costs.

## Key Features
- Categorize expenses by type
- Record payment details
- Track vendor information
- Link machines used for the expense
- Calculate total costs per module

## Available In
- Crops
- Fields
- Livestock
- Machines
- Farms

## Tips
- Record expenses immediately after purchase to avoid missing entries
- Take photos of receipts and attach them using Documents
- Categorize expenses consistently for better reporting
- Select the tractor/implement used to build machine usage history

## Best Practices
- Keep all receipts and attach them digitally
- Record labor costs including casual workers
- Track both cash and credit purchases
- Link machines to expenses for usage tracking
