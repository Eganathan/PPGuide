# Yields Action

Track harvest quantities and production output.

## Purpose
Record crop harvests, milk production, egg collection, and other measurable outputs.

## Key Features
- Record quantities harvested
- Track quality grades
- Monitor production trends
- Compare yields across seasons

## Available In
- Crops
- Livestock

## Tips
- Record yields on harvest day for accuracy
- Include quality information to track premium vs regular produce
- Compare yields across seasons to identify improvements

## Best Practices
- Use consistent units of measurement
- Record both quantity and quality metrics
- Note any factors that affected the yield (weather, inputs, timing)
