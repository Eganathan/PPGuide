# Welcome to Personal Pannai

Your complete farm management companion. Personal Pannai helps you track crops, livestock, machinery, inventory, and finances all in one app.

---

## Core Modules

### Crops & Fields
Track your crops from planting to harvest. Manage multiple seasons, monitor growth stages, and associate crops with specific fields.

[Learn about Crops →](/en/docs/modules/crops/)

### Livestock
Manage cattle, poultry, goats, sheep, and pigs. Track individually for dairy cattle or manage as groups.

[Learn about Livestock →](/en/docs/modules/livestock/)

### Machinery & Inventory
Monitor your tractors, implements, and all farm inputs like seeds, fertilizers, and fuel.

[Learn about Machines →](/en/docs/modules/machines/)

---

## Getting Started

New to Personal Pannai? Follow these steps:

1. **Set Up Your Farm** - Create your farm profile with basic details
2. **Add Your Fields** - Register all your agricultural fields
3. **Create Crops or Livestock** - Start tracking your farming activities
4. **Record Activities** - Log expenses, income, tasks, and notes
5. **Review Progress** - Check your dashboard and summaries

[Get Started →](/en/docs/getting-started/)

---

## Key Features

- **Crop Management** - Track 7 growth stages from preparation to sale
- **Field Tracking** - Maintain crop rotation history and field records
- **Livestock Management** - Individual tracking for dairy cattle
- **Machinery Logs** - Usage reports and maintenance schedules
- **Inventory Control** - Stock management with cost tracking
- **Financial Records** - Income, expenses, and profitability analysis
- **Team Collaboration** - Role-based access for owners, managers, and workers
