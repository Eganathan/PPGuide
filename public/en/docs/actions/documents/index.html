    Documents Action#

    Attach files, photos, and documents to your records.

    Purpose#

    Store receipts, photos, certificates, manuals, and any other relevant files with your farming records.

    Key Features#

    • Attach photos and images
    • Store PDF documents
    • Keep receipts digitally
    • Organize files by module

    Available In#

    • Crops
    • Fields
    • Livestock
    • Machines
    • Farms

    Tips#

    • Take photos of crop growth stages for visual progress tracking
    • Photograph receipts immediately before they fade
    • Store important documents like land papers and insurance policies

    Best Practices#

    • Use clear, well-lit photos
    • Name or tag documents descriptively
    • Regularly back up important documents
