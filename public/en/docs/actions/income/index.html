    Income Action#

    Record all revenue and sales from your farming activities.

    Purpose#

    Track money earned from selling crops, livestock products, milk, eggs, or animals.

    Key Features#

    • Record sale amounts and dates
    • Track buyer information
    • Link sales to contacts for transaction tracking
    • Monitor quantities sold
    • Calculate total earnings per module

    Available In#

    • Crops
    • Livestock

    Contact Integration#

    Select a buyer contact when recording income to build a transaction history with that contact. Sales linked to contacts appear in the contact detail screen, helping you track your biggest buyers and total sales to each.

    Tips#

    • Record sales immediately to maintain accurate income records
    • Always select the buyer contact to build transaction history
    • Include buyer details for future reference and repeat sales
    • Track partial sales separately to monitor selling patterns

    Best Practices#

    • Record income on the same day as the sale
    • Include quantity and rate for accurate per-unit calculations
    • Link to buyer contact for relationship tracking
    • Add notes for special arrangements or payment terms
