    Notes Action#

    Record observations, comments, and important information.

    Purpose#

    Document anything noteworthy about your crops, animals, equipment, or fields that doesn’t fit other categories.

    Key Features#

    • Free-form text entries
    • Date-stamped records
    • Searchable notes history
    • Link to specific modules

    Available In#

    • Crops
    • Fields
    • Livestock
    • Machines
    • Farms

    Tips#

    • Record weather observations that affect your farming
    • Document pest or disease sightings immediately
    • Note successful techniques for future reference

    Best Practices#

    • Be specific and include relevant details
    • Record observations the same day they occur
    • Use consistent terminology for easy searching later
