    Farm Modules#

    Personal Pannai organizes your farm data into modules. Each module focuses on a specific aspect of your farming operation.

    Available Modules#

    Module Description
    Crops Track crops from planting to harvest with season management
    Fields Manage your agricultural fields and crop rotation history
    Livestock Manage cattle, poultry, goats, sheep, pigs with optional individual tracking
    Machines Track tractors, implements, usage, and maintenance
    Inventory Manage seeds, fertilizers, fuel, and other farm inputs
    Contacts Organize vendors, buyers, and track transaction history
    Roles Manage team access with Owner, Manager, and Worker roles

    How Modules Work Together#

    Modules in Personal Pannai are interconnected:

    • Crops link to Fields to track which areas are being cultivated
    • Expenses on any module can link to Machines and Inventory items
    • Contacts automatically track purchases and sales across modules
    • Roles control who can access data in each module
