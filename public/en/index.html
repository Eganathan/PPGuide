    Welcome to Pannai#

    Your complete farm management companion. Pannai helps you track crops, livestock, machinery, inventory, and finances all in one app.

    New to farm tracking? Read From Guessing to Knowing: A Farmer’s Journey to see how data transforms farming.

    Core Modules#

    Farm#

    Create and manage your farm profile. Set up farm details, manage team members, and track farm-level expenses and activities.

    Learn about Farm →

    Crops#

    Track your crops from planting to harvest. Manage multiple seasons, monitor growth stages, and record all related activities throughout each season.

    Learn about Crops →

    Fields#

    Manage your agricultural fields and track their usage history. Associate fields with crops, maintain crop rotation records, and store field-specific activities.

    Learn about Fields →

    Livestock#

    Manage cattle, poultry, goats, sheep, and pigs. Track individually for dairy cattle or manage as groups.

    Learn about Livestock →

    Machines#

    Track your tractors, implements, and other farm equipment. Record usage reports and maintain service schedules.

    Learn about Machines →

    Inventory#

    Manage all your farm inputs including seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, fuel, and spare parts. Track stock levels and link usage to expenses.

    Learn about Inventory →

    Getting Started#

    New to Pannai? Follow these steps:

    1. Set Up Your Farm - Create your farm profile with basic details
    2. Add Your Fields - Register all your agricultural fields
    3. Create Crops or Livestock - Start tracking your farming activities
    4. Record Activities - Log expenses, income, tasks, and notes
    5. Review Progress - Check your dashboard and summaries

    Get Started →

    Key Features#

    • Crop Management - Track 7 growth stages from preparation to sale
    • Field Tracking - Maintain crop rotation history and field records
    • Livestock Management - Individual tracking for dairy cattle
    • Machinery Logs - Usage reports and maintenance schedules
    • Inventory Control - Stock management with cost tracking
    • Financial Records - Income, expenses, and profitability analysis
    • Team Collaboration - Role-based access for owners, managers, and workers
